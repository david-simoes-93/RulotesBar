Our service

Rental

Rental of Trailers and Vans for Street Food, showcooking, events, parties, festivals, Catering support, private parties, concerts, sport events, marketing, brand promotion, product launch, Food Styling, mobile kitchen, among others.

Minimum of 3-day rental, except for special cases. Equipments are only guaranteed after the reservation value has been payed. We deliver and pick-up at the event's location.

Note: We do not rent vehicles without driver.

Quality

Equipment in good state, hygienic, and nice looking. All equipment includes HACCP, as well as Hygiene-Sanitation Certification.

During the rental period, the renter is responsible for: hygiene, quality and legality of products on sale, any damages caused to the trailer during the event, billing equipment, activity permits, among others.

Customization

The rental does not include support equipment: tables, chairs, wastebaskets, electrical extensions, cleaning supplies, consumables, etc. We can include equipments like grilling plates, electric stoves, ovens, industrial fryers, etc. Gas-operated equipments are not allowed.

Equipment must be returned in the same state they were rented. It is possible to customize the trailer/van for each case, as long as the materials used can be easily removed, and the vehicle is returned without lasting traces of the customization.
